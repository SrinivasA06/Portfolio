Research

MDPI Engineering Proceedings  |  Vol. 34, No. 1  |  March 2023  |  Open Access  |  DOI: 10.3390/HMAM2-14158

Advanced Driver Fatigue Detection by Integration of OpenCV DNN Module and Deep Learning

A comparative study of four face detection algorithms — Viola-Jones, Dlib, MTCNN, and OpenCV DNN — applied to real-time driver drowsiness monitoring. A modified convolutional neural network classifier was developed and trained on the Kaggle Drowsiness Dataset, achieving 96.8% accuracy and outperforming four prior state-of-the-art methods. The OpenCV DNN module demonstrated superior performance in handling occlusion, fast head movements, and side-face detection at 46 frames per second. Presented at the International Conference on Holography Meets Advanced Manufacturing, Institute of Physics, University of Tartu, Estonia, February 2023. Co-authored with researchers from KL University, Bharath Institute, and Reva University.
